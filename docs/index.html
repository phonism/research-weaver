How It Works

Understanding the research process from query to comprehensive report

1

Query Analysis

Supervisor agent analyzes your research query and breaks it down into specialized research tasks.

2

Agent Creation

Specialized researcher agents are created with specific roles and expertise areas relevant to your query.

3

Information Gathering

Agents search the web, read articles, and extract relevant information using advanced AI techniques.

4

Analysis & Synthesis

Collected information is analyzed, cross-referenced, and synthesized into comprehensive insights.

5

Report Generation

A final research report is generated with citations, structured analysis, and actionable conclusions.
